Unwanted Subletting Causes Problems

Disturbed Residents

Residents feel uncomfortable with strangers constantly coming and going with no prior background check.

Increased Liability

Property managers may be liable for accidents that occur on their property; accidents that insurance might not cover when due to an illegal tenant.

Damaged Property

Illegal subletters don't feel responsible for the property, which often leads to greater property wear and tear.

Proactively stop these problems before they occur. Sublet Patrol monitors rental sites for suspicious postings and notifies you as soon as one is found. This stops the problem at the source, before a guest is even able to stay on your property. Our proprietary algorithm not only saves you time, but also increases resident satisfaction and mitigates risk. Please contact us to learn how this solution can work for you.

Weekly Reports

Our software provides weekly reports of suspicious rental listings in the area, allowing management to follow up with residents and stop the problem at the source.

Historic Log of Resident Violations

Receiving reports frequently provides a long history of repeat offenders. This is extremely useful when trying to prove consistent lease violations.
