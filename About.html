SubletPatrol Services

SubletPatrol allows you to proactively stop illegal subletting before it occurs. Sublet Patrol monitors rental sites for postings within your area and notifies you as soon as one is found. This stops the problem at the source, before a guest is even able to stay on your property. Our service not only saves you time, but also increases resident satisfaction and mitigates risk.

Services Designed to Fit Any Sized Apartment

Weekly Reports

Weekly reports of suspicious rental listings in your area give you regular and frequent updates to ensure that all issues are caught and handled in a timely manner.

Historic Log of Resident Violations

All reports are saved, providing a long history of any repeat offenders. This is extremely useful when trying to prove consistent lease violations.

Automated Monitoring

Our service ensures that no short-term rental listing is missed and that they are all sent to you in a timely and consistant manner. 

Issues Solved By Our Service

The effects of illegal subletting on your property can be broad and far reaching. The management, property, and residents can all feel its effects.

Resident Turnover

It's no secret that good residents are expensive to lose, but unfortunately they are the first ones to leave when issues of illegal subletting arise.

Increased Liability

Property managers may be liable for accidents that occur on their property; accidents that insurance might not cover when due to an illegal tenant.

Property Damage

Short-term rental sites guests are often on vacation and don't feel responsible for the property, which often leads to greater property wear and tear.

Administrative Costs

Apartment staff members now have to dedicate a significant amount of time to monitoring rental sites and looking out for unwanted guests.
